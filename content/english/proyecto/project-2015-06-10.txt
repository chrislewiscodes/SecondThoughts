The Second Thoughts Series is an annual program of lectures, workshops, exhibitions, publications of artists and designers. It is the first educational initiative in Mexico focused on critical graphic design practices; each issue will consist of a series of visits by international artists and designers to mexico city and will culminate with an exhibition and a publication that compiles the work of invited artists to document what happened during the year.

The activities of this first edition will be held in cultural institutions of the city of Mexico between fall 2015 and spring 2016.
