Second Thoughts is a program of lectures, workshops and panel discussions; focused on artistic practices that take advantage of visual comunication as a resource and reason for experimentation and critical research. With duration of one year each edition consists of a series of visits from international artists to Mexico city, and culminates with an exhibition and a publication, bringing together the work of the guests with that of local artists

The activities will take place at the premises of alumnos47 and the Tamayo museum and will start from november 2015
